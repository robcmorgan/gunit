## Goodreads best for Teens

Goodreads has a list of the best books for teens and young adults, voted for by readers.

#### [The Lightning Thief](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/1689)
[![](covers/1689.jpg)](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/1689)
##### mythical · fast-paced · humorous
##### Rick Riordan
##### Fantasy, 2005, 377 pages

Mythology crashes headlong into modern Americana. Riordan treats adolescent angst as a literal battlefield, delivering a narrative that never takes its foot off the gas while demanding readers care about its profoundly relatable cast.

Dyslexia and ADHD aren't learning disabilities for Percy Jackson—they are hardwired combat instincts. Finding out his father is an Olympian god is just the start of his bad week, kicking off a desperate cross-country road trip to track down Zeus's stolen master bolt before the world burns.


#### [City of Bones](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/1690)
[![](covers/1690.jpg)](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/1690)
##### urban · gritty · supernatural
##### Cassandra Clare
##### Fantasy, 2007, 485 pages

Shadows hide claws, and glamour masks fangs. Clare constructs an intricate, gritty urban underworld layered directly beneath the streets of New York, demanding attention through sheer atmospheric weight and razor-sharp ensemble banter.

Welcome to the Pandemonium Club, where a casual murder goes unnoticed by everyone except Clary Fray. Suddenly thrust into the path of tattooed, lethal warriors dedicated to exterminating demons, Clary must unlock her own wiped memories to save her mother from an ancient enemy.


#### [Eclipse](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/300)
[![](covers/300.jpg)](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/300)
##### brooding · romantic · tense
##### Stephenie Meyer
##### Romance, 2007, 629 pages

Tension builds like a tightening wire. Meyer shifts away from quiet melancholy into the high-stakes arena of shifting alliances and cold-blooded vendettas, anchoring the claustrophobic emotional triangulation with impending violence.

Seattle bleeds under a string of unsolved, savage killings orchestrated by a newborn vampire army. Survival demands the unthinkable, forcing sworn enemies—Edward's coven and Jacob's werewolf pack—to fight shoulder-to-shoulder, even as Bella's heart threatens to tear their fragile truce apart.


#### Eragon
##### epic · traditional · heroic
##### Christopher Paolini
##### Fantasy, 2003, 509 pages

High fantasy in its purest, most unabashed form. Paolini honors the genre's grandest traditions, mapping out a sprawling continent rich with invented languages, ancient grudges, and the majestic bond forged between boy and beast.

It was supposed to be just a blue stone, a lucky find in the Spine mountains that could pay for winter provisions. Then it hatched. Overnight, a simple farm boy becomes the first Dragon Rider in a generation, hunted by an empire run by a mad, immortal king.


#### [Catching Fire](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/849)
[![](covers/849.jpg)](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/849)
##### dystopian · defiant · brutal
##### Suzanne Collins
##### Sci-Fi, 2009, 391 pages

Dread saturates every page. This is not merely a survival narrative, but a surgical dismantling of totalitarian propaganda and the trauma of televised violence, refusing to let its protagonist—or the audience—rest.

The Capitol is watching, and President Snow is furious. Katniss Everdeen's desperate act with the nightlock berries ignited a spark across the districts, and now the rules of the 75th Hunger Games are being viciously rewritten to ensure the girl on fire burns to ash.


#### [The Elephant Tree](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/1692)
[![](covers/1692.jpg)](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/1692)
##### gritty · psychological · bleak
##### R.D. Ronald
##### Thriller, 2010, 320 pages

Unforgiving and brutally precise. Ronald strips away any romanticized veneer from the criminal underworld, exposing a raw nerve of desperation and moral decay that actively defies redemption.

Plunge into the suffocating grip of an illicit drug syndicate. Scott and Angela are drowning, and their desperate bid to carve out an independent operation might be the very thing that buries them under a mountain of paranoia and betrayal.


#### [Uglies](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/1693)
[![](covers/1693.jpg)](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/1693)
##### satirical · thought-provoking · fast
##### Scott Westerfeld
##### Sci-Fi, 2005, 425 pages

Conformity is carved with a scalpel. Westerfeld delivers a biting lens on cultural vanity and the weaponization of beauty standards, moving at a relentless pace to dissect the terrifying concept of engineered complacency.

Ugly is a temporary condition. At sixteen, your bone structure is corrected, and you are unleashed into the dizzying paradise of New Pretty Town—until Tally’s best friend goes rogue, leaving Tally to infiltrate a rebel camp or remain hideous forever.


#### [Breaking Dawn](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/251)
[![](covers/251.jpg)](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/251)
##### climactic · controversial · dramatic
##### Stephenie Meyer
##### Romance, 2008, 756 pages

A honeymoon, a horrifyingly accelerated pregnancy, and a death sentence. Word of Bella’s forbidden immortal child has reached the ruling Volturi, sparking a desperate, worldwide rally of vampires to stand against the impending slaughter in Forks.


#### [New Moon](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/114)

##### melancholic · agonizing · quiet
##### Stephenie Meyer

#### [The Chronicles of Narnia](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/868)
##### allegorical · wondrous · timeless
##### C.S. Lewis
##### Fantasy, 1950, 767 pages

Step backward through the fur coats and leave the Blitz behind. A snow-covered lamppost marks the threshold to Narnia, a land enslaved in perpetual winter where four displaced siblings must rally behind a legendary, untamed lion to break the ice.

#### [Ender’s Game](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/847)

#### [Holes](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/1695)

##### Louis Sachar

#### [City of Glass](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/1696)

##### Cassandra Clare

#### [The Giver](https://books.rob.me.uk/book/1697)

##### Lois Lowry
##### Sci-Fi, 1993, 240 pages
