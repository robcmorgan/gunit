## How to use the library

- This is a shared library of books on the server.
- You can browse and download books using a phone or a laptop.
- You can also browse and download from the Kindle (though Kindles are a bit slow)
- You've also got your personal 'forKindle' shelf in the library. You can add things to the shelf and then sync it to the Kindle.


# The basic idea here:
### Step 1. add books from the library the the "forKindle" shelf
### Step 2. go to the 'sync' option on the Kindle and sync the shelf
### Step 3. then you can add new books to the library by going to "requests" 

----

## Requesting new books

You can ask the server to get most books and (*provided it's all working*) they should show up automatically after about 5 minutes.

Go to "request a book from the server" and select it here

![Select](/screenshots/select.png)

You probably want one of the first options. epubs are good. PDFs are bad (they don't display well on a Kindle)

![Options](/screenshots/listofoptions.png)

## On the Kindle

You'll almost always want to be inside of an app called KOReader. This one app is your Kindle now and you shouldn't need to come out of it ever.
You can see all the books that are on the Kindle fairly easily. As for adding....

#### Getting to the library from the Kindle

Go to the search (magnifying glass) icon at the top menu, then select **OPDS catalog**.

![Downloading](/screenshots/menu_for_opds.jpg)

The **Gunit library (browse)** option there will let you browse and directly download books to your home folder here.

> You can also try the free libraries there - apparently "Standard Ebooks" is good for the classics.

#### Syncing the shelf

The last option there is the Synced shelf. Tough word, synced. This downloads everyong on your shelf to your Kindle if you're on wifi. It doesn't do it automatically, though, sadly, I think you have to hold down on the menu item and select Sync like this.

![Edit](/screenshots/edit.jpg)

#### If you can't see the books after downloading them

Kindle (even not jailbroken) doesn't always recognise it's got the book it has downloaded. Easiest way to wake it up is to select the home icon on the top left here.

![Edit](/screenshots/home_icon.jpg)
